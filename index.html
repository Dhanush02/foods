What We Have



    Wedding Cakes

Specifically designed for each couple, our customized cakes exemplify our
attention to detail, beauty & taste. If you are planning a wedding, our
cakes will be a perfect addition to your event.

    Sculpted Cakes

Sculpted cakes are cakes that have been sculpted and carefully crafted
into shapes (like shoes, pillows, animals or even people), covered in
buttercream and fondant and then painted or decorated with edible food
colors and decorations. These could be birthday, baby shower, or even
wedding cakes. These cakes, at first sight, are often mistaken for the real
thing and we hear “That’s a cake?!” a lot.

    Celebration Cakes

We also offer a wide variety of celebration cakes for birthdays,
graduation parties, and other types of events. All our cakes are baked
freshly on-site to order, with only the freshest ingredients to ensure that
your cake tastes as wonderful as it looks!
